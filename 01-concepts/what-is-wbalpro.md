# What is WbalPro?

WbalPro is a data field for Garmin watches that turns your watch into an advanced running power meter — without needing an external footpod or sensor. It uses data already available in your watch: GPS, barometer, and heart rate monitor.

The app shows real-time running metrics during your activity, including power output in Watts, W'bal (your anaerobic energy reserve), grade adjusted pace, and more.

WbalPro is designed for runners who want to train smarter, pace their races more precisely, and understand their performance at a deeper level.

**Also known as:** W'bal Pro, Wbal Pro, running power data field, anaerobic battery app for Garmin.
