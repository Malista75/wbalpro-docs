# W'bal — Your Real-Time Anaerobic Battery Level

## What is W'bal?

W'bal (W' Balance) is the real-time display of how much anaerobic energy you have left at any given moment during your run. It starts at your full W' (e.g., 18 kJ) and changes continuously based on your effort.

- Running above CP → W'bal goes DOWN (you are spending anaerobic energy)
- Running below CP → W'bal goes UP (you are recovering)

The battery icon on the WbalPro screen is a visual representation of your W'bal.

## What do the numbers mean?

- **Full W'bal (e.g., 18 kJ):** You are fully fresh anaerobically. You can sprint, climb hard, or push.
- **50% W'bal (e.g., 9 kJ):** You have used half your reserve. Still plenty, but manage effort.
- **20% or less:** Getting critical. Avoid sustained efforts above CP.
- **0 kJ:** Anaerobic battery depleted. Maintaining pace above CP is no longer possible. Slow down immediately.

## Why does W'bal recover?

When you run below CP, your body replenishes W'. The recovery is not instant — it is gradual and depends on how far below CP you are running. The lower the intensity, the faster the recharge.

**Also known as:** W prime balance, anaerobic battery, energy reserve, Wbal battery level, anaerobic energy left.
