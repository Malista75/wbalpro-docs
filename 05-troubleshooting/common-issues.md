# Troubleshooting WbalPro: Common Problems and Solutions

## W'bal shows "---" or 0 at the start

**Cause:** CP or W' values are not set, or set to 0 in the app settings.
**Solution:** Open Garmin Connect → Connect IQ Apps → WbalPro Settings. Enter valid CP and W' values. Sync your watch.

## W'bal drops to 0 immediately at low effort

**Cause:** CP is set too high. WbalPro thinks you are always above your threshold.
**Solution:** Lower your CP value. A 20-minute time trial at max sustainable effort (×0.95) is a reliable estimate.

## W'bal never moves — stays full the whole run

**Cause:** CP is set too low, or W' is set extremely high.
**Solution:** Increase CP or decrease W' to better reflect your real capacity.

## The data field does not appear on my watch

**Cause:** Sync did not complete, or the data field was not added to the activity profile.
**Solution:**
1. Open Garmin Connect and force a manual sync
2. Restart your watch
3. Go to Settings → Activity Profiles → Running → Data Screens and add WbalPro manually

## Power shows 0 or very low values

**Cause:** GPS signal not acquired, or the activity just started and GPS is still locking.
**Solution:** Wait for a solid GPS lock before starting the activity. Walk outside for 30–60 seconds before starting your run.

## The screen layout looks different from the manual

**Cause:** You may have an older version of WbalPro installed.
**Solution:** Check the Connect IQ Store for an available update. Some metric labels changed across versions (e.g., "Apwr" → "Total Power", "P180" → "P3").

## How to reset and reinstall

1. Open Garmin Connect → Connect IQ Apps
2. Find WbalPro and tap **Uninstall**
3. Restart your watch
4. Reinstall from the Connect IQ Store
5. Reconfigure your CP and W' settings
