# Installing WbalPro on Your Garmin Watch

## Step 1: Find WbalPro in the Connect IQ Store

1. Open the **Garmin Connect IQ Store** on your phone or at apps.garmin.com
2. Search for **"WbalPro"** or **"W'bal Pro"**
3. Tap the app and select **Install**

## Step 2: Sync to your watch

After installing, sync your Garmin watch with the Garmin Connect app. The data field will be transferred to your watch automatically.

## Step 3: Add the data field to an activity

1. On your watch, go to **Settings → Activity Profiles**
2. Select the activity (e.g., Running)
3. Go to **Data Screens → Add New Screen**
4. Choose **Data Field** and select **WbalPro** from the list

## Compatible devices

WbalPro is compatible with Garmin watches that support Connect IQ data fields with GPS and barometer access. This includes most Forerunner, Fenix, Epix, and Vívoactive series watches that support Connect IQ 3.0 or higher.

If you are unsure whether your watch is compatible, check the Connect IQ Store page for WbalPro — it lists all supported devices.

**Troubleshooting installation:** If the data field does not appear after syncing, try restarting your watch and re-syncing via Garmin Connect.
