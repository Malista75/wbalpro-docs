# Configuring WbalPro: Setting Your CP and W'

## Why configuration matters

WbalPro calculates your W'bal using two personal values: your Critical Power (CP) and your W' Prime. Without accurate values, the battery display will not reflect your real physiological state.

## How to enter your CP and W'

1. Open the **Garmin Connect** app on your phone
2. Go to **More → Connect IQ Apps**
3. Find **WbalPro** in your installed apps and tap **Settings**
4. Enter your **CP** value in Watts (e.g., 280)
5. Enter your **W'** value in kilojoules (e.g., 18)
6. Save and sync your watch

## What values should I use?

- **CP:** If you do not have a lab test, use 95% of your average power from a maximal 20-minute run.
- **W':** A reasonable starting estimate for a trained runner is 15–20 kJ. Adjust over time based on how the battery behaves during intervals.

## What happens if my values are wrong?

- **CP too high:** W'bal will drop too fast, making efforts feel harder than they are on screen.
- **CP too low:** W'bal will barely drop even at high effort — the battery will seem unrealistically full.
- **W' too high:** The battery percentage will appear fuller than it really is.
- **W' too low:** The battery will hit 0 too quickly during normal hard efforts.

Recalibrate periodically as your fitness changes.
