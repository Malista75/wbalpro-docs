# What This Support Agent Can and Cannot Answer

## This agent can help you with

- Understanding what each metric in WbalPro means
- Setting up and configuring WbalPro on your Garmin watch
- Interpreting W'bal, power, GAP, VO2, PF5, P3/P10/P20 during your runs
- Troubleshooting common issues (missing data, wrong values, installation problems)
- Understanding the concepts of Critical Power (CP) and W' Prime

## This agent cannot help you with

- The internal algorithms or proprietary calculations used by WbalPro — these are not publicly documented
- Modifying the app or requesting features
- Garmin hardware issues unrelated to WbalPro
- Medical or training advice beyond what is explained in this guide

## Need more help?

For questions beyond what this agent can answer, contact WbalPro support directly.
