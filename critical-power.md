# Critical Power (CP) — Your Sustainable Power Threshold

## What is Critical Power?

Critical Power (CP) is the maximum power output you can sustain for a very long time without fatiguing anaerobically. Think of it as your "aerobic ceiling" — the line between efforts you can sustain and efforts that drain your anaerobic battery.

- Below CP: your body recovers. You can keep going for a long time.
- Above CP: you are spending anaerobic energy (W'). This is finite and will run out.

CP is expressed in Watts (W) and is personal — it depends on your fitness level.

## How do I find my CP?

A common field estimate: run a 20-minute all-out time trial and take 95% of your average power as your CP estimate. More accurate protocols use multiple tests at different durations (e.g., 3 min, 12 min, 20 min).

## Where do I set my CP in WbalPro?

You enter your CP value in the WbalPro settings inside Garmin Connect. See the "Initial Setup" document for step-by-step instructions.

**Also known as:** critical power threshold, CP, aerobic ceiling, sustainable power, FTP equivalent for running.
